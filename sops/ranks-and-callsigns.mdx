### STG Ranks and Callsigns

| Rank        | Callsign |
| ----------- | -------- |
| Commander   | STG 0    |
| Team Leader | STG 1-2  |
| Operator    | STG 3-18 |

**STG Callsigns during pages and Overt Ops, HLX Callsigns during Covert Ops.**

**If the Commander is on LOA, a Team Leader will act as Tactical Commander.**
